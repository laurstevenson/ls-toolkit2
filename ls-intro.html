Getting Started

Let's begin with the basics. As a Learning Specialist, your role and responsibilities are:

  • To review all documentation, understand the impact of functional limitations, and assess a student's disability-related needs and how these may impact their academic or campus experience
  • To establish reasonable and individualized academic accommodation plans which may include learning strategies, exam and teaching adapted measures
  • To support students, professors, and other support staff to ensure that everyone's requirements are met and provide the best chance of academic success
  • To provide pertinent information about services and supports available to students with disabilities
