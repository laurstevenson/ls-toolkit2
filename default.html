Learning Specialist Toolkit

As a Learning Specialist, your role and responsibilities are essential to the Academic Accommodation process. As part of the onboarding experience, the LS Toolkit serves as a comprehensive guide aimed at accelerating new hire impact.

Say hello to a modern way to host documentation. Goodbye the days of outdated and inaccessible handover binders!

Learning Objectives

Understanding your role and responsibilities as a Learning Specialist. But most importantly, providing students the best chance of academic success.
Performing needs assessments and understanding what may impact a student's academic or campus experience.
Establishing individualized and reasonable academic accommodation plans that may include: Exam, Teaching, and Learning supports.
Knowing when to partner with stakeholders to discuss supports for graduate programs and work placement requirements
Mastering the the different intake appointments, navigating Ventus, and building your own Learning Specialist toolkit

Ready to get started?

  1. Begin by getting familiar with Ventus, our in-house accommodation software, and the basics of the LS role
  2. Learn documentation requirements and best practices in preparation for the intake meeting
  3. Next, deep dive into the intake workflow, the express versus traditional intake, questions to ask, and setting up the accommodation and learning plan
  4. Finally, what goes on after the intake meeting and next steps
